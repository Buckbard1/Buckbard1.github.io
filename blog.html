My Latest Writings

I frequently share my thoughts on my Medium channel (feed on the left). These essays will center around designing the future. Our history may already be written but our future has an infinite number of possible branches. Creativity and design thinkning are two powerfull tools in the toolkit of life. I will devote the rest of my life to mastering those tools and sharing them with others.

I have enjoyed sharing my experiences in hopes that others can learn something from them. Some of the greatest events in my early career was when older colleagues would share stories. I'm a true believer that if we don't learn from our past we are doomed to constantly repeat it. I encourage you all to share your own stories in the comments.

Now I'm going to pivot from the past to the future. I will use my voice to share ideas, tips, and tricks for us all to develop the skills needed to design better outcomes for ourselves. Because all of our individual threads will weave our collective human future.

I hope you will learn with me.

"If you don't design your future, someone or something else will design it for you"
- Edward de Bono

 

The Canary Works Vision Workshop is an energizing and dynamic event where leaders and teams come together to reimagine the future of their teams. Step into a collaborative space buzzing with creativity and high energy as you engage in deep discussions, breakout sessions, and interactive activities designed to ignite innovation. Guided by our expert facilitators, learners are pushed to think boldly, align on core values, and co-create a powerful shared vision for the future.

The atmosphere is one of inspiration and focus, with teams mapping out long-term strategies, identifying unexpected opportunities, and setting stretch goals. The workshop seamlessly blends blue-sky thinking with actionable planning, resulting in a compelling roadmap for growth and success. It's a day filled with visionary insights, powerful breakthroughs, and a collective momentum that drives the company forward with renewed purpose and direction.

Guided by proven Futurism tools, this workshop answers the following questions:

  •    How do teams and individuals align to the company vision?
  •    How can we think out of the box, beyond the day to day?
  •    How can we use forward thinking tools to advance and achieve our own goals?
  •    How do we measure success?
  •    How can we love the work we do again?
  •    What gets us excited about our work?

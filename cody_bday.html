If you’re a knowledge worker, make no mistake, AI is coming for your job.

Investors and tech giants aren’t pouring hundreds of billions of dollars into developing machine learning to make you a little more efficient at your job. That’s not the outsized ROI everyone is chasing. Still, most people cling to that. And I don’t blame them - they’re scared for their livelihoods and families.

When automation came for blue collar work most people said it wouldn’t impact them. Now we have millions of Americans who are unemployed and poor, just waiting out their lives in a bitter fog.

Smart people prepare. Smart people have contingency and emergency plans. Smart people anticipate and pivot.

You can choose to hope AI doesn’t impact you. And honestly, if you’re less than 5 years from full retirement you’re probably right. Everyone else needs to prepare.
